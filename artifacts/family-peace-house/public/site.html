Welcome to Family Peace House

A guesthouse where guests
become family.

Tucked away on a quiet pedestrian lane in the heart of Thamel, Family Peace House is a peaceful retreat from the city's buzz. Personally managed by our owner Bimal and his team, we welcome travelers from around the world with warm Nepali hospitality, freshly cooked breakfasts under prayer flags, and everything you need to fall in love with Kathmandu.
